# SparkMIT — Agentic Serendipity for MIT's Elite

**SparkMIT** ignites free moments into epic, affinity-matched connections for MIT's sharpest minds (Harvard guests for MIT-only events). AI agents (opt-in, human-controlled) scan calendars, phenotype personalities, and propose high-value meets—study syncs, coffee clashes, GTM deep-dives, mindful walks. It maps *where* genius happens, powers value exchanges (coffee for code review), and feels intuitive, not "AI creepy." The next Facebook: agentic, safe, soul-aligned.

***

## Hero Pitch

MIT life = grind + glory + isolation. SparkMIT fixes that. Verified MIT/Harvard .edu users grant *optional* calendar read access. Agents detect free slots, match on **affinity traits** (Big Five science), and suggest: "Alex (CSAIL, High Openness like you) free 7-9pm? Study 6.036 + pizza?"

Post-meet, it learns: *where* breakthroughs happen (Stata Center whiteboards? Kendall coffee?), *what* works (60-min structured vs freeform?), and surfaces patterns. Freemium scales to "SparkEvents" for group hacks. Your NFX FAST thesis, agentic-ified.

## Affinity Science: Why It Works

**Big Five (OCEAN)**—gold standard psychology—powers matching. 4 traits predict 80%+ of connection success:


| Trait | Measures | Drives | High-Scorers Love |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Openness** | Curiosity, wild ideas | Intellectual fireworks | Brainstorms, paper dissections |
| **Conscientiousness** | Reliability, organization | Trust, accountability | Study syncs, deadlines |
| **Extraversion** | Social energy | Pace matching | Coffee clashes, group hangs |
| **Agreeableness** | Empathy, cooperation | Harmony, support | Mentoring, value exchanges |

**10-Question Vibe Check** (5-point scale):

1. "I love exploring wild, out-there ideas" (Openness)
2. "New experiences > routine" (Openness)
3. "I get energy from people" (Extraversion)
4. "Big groups sound fun" (Extraversion)
5. "I always deliver on promises" (Conscientiousness)
6. "Organization = winning" (Conscientiousness)
7. "Others' feelings guide me" (Agreeableness)
8. "Team wins > solo wins" (Agreeableness)
9. "I stay chill under pressure" (Low Neuroticism)
10. "Drama drains me" (Low Neuroticism)

**Matching**: 70%+ similarity → core matches. Strategic complements (High-Open + High-Agreeable) for growth.
